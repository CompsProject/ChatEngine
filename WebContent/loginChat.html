If you haven't read this before, please read this in its entirety!

If you have read this before, at least skim over the bold/underlined words since the system changes frequently!

There are now 2 windows in the chat page.

The Discussion Window is where you will chat with your other team members.

The Answer Window is where you will type the final answer for your group. The answer must be submitted by the whole group to the TA.


When you are ready to submit your answer to the TA, all group members must agree and click Submit on the sidebar. There is a sidebar on the right that shows your group members and the TA for your group. Those whose names are blinking are currently ready to submit the answer. You can choose to Submit by clicking your own name OR clicking the Submit button.

Once the whole group has agreed to submit, the final answer cannot be edited unless everyone cancels or the TA reviews your answer. To cancel a submission, click your name or the Cancel button.

As soon as the final answer has been submitted, the TA will be alerted. The TA will review your answer by approving or declining. If approved, you can go to the next question, otherwise you will have to discuss with your team to get the right answer.

The aim of this lab is collaboration. Remember that you are graded on your participation so make sure to keep involved, ask your team if you are confused and help others if they are. Keep all communication in the chat, stay focused and have fun!!
